PB&J

The completed sandwich

This will teach you have to make the most delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich ever!

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly (your favorite kind)
  • Unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Take out two slices of your sliced bread
  2. Heat up a pan with a table spoon of butter
  3. Toast both sides of the bread in the pan
  4. Take out both slices from the pan
  5. Apply peanut butter to one side of one slice and your preferred jelly to the other slice
  6. Close the sandwich and enjoy!
